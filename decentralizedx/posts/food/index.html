All the Pieces

Here are all the pieces required to operate a food delivery service:

Client App(s)

This is required for discovery of all the restaurants/food providers around the area. They integrate checkout/payment options and provide order flow, they differentiate on the basis of selection they offer and the partners they integrate on the back-end

Listing Partners

They are responsible for aggregating restaurant information on-ground. Restaurant KYC, Menu creation, Price listing are all done in accordance to protocols. They get paid by client apps for sharing their information. They differentiate on the basis of the depth + quality of their catalog.

Logistics Providers
